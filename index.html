Scout: Who's Raising?

Like other founders, I'm often approached by entrepreneurs looking for help raising their next round. If they’re good, I introduce them to my investors. Most closed venture deals start out like this – they’re network referrals from people investors trust.

As an investor, you miss out on a lot of deals simply because you don’t find out about them. They’re never even referred to you because they're outside your network.

Scout combines vetted high-quality introductions from founders all over the valley into a single stream. Strong entrepreneurs get in front of more investors and you won't miss any deals. Everybody wins.
